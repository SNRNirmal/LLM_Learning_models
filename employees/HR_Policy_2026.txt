REMOTE WORK POLICY 2026
Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. Eligibility
All full-time employees who have completed their probation period are eligible for the Hybrid Remote Work program. Contractors and interns are eligible upon manager approval.

2. Hybrid Model
The standard model requires employees to be in the office 2 days a week (Tuesday and Thursday). The remaining 3 days can be worked remotely from any location within the country.

3. Work Hours
Core hours are 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM local time. Employees must be available on Slack and Zoom during these hours. Flexible scheduling is allowed outside core hours.

4. Equipment Stipend
A one-time stipend of $1,000 is provided for setting up a home office. This can be used for monitors, ergonomic chairs, or high-speed internet installation.

5. Data Security
All remote work must be conducted on company-issued laptops. Use of personal devices for accessing the internal VPN is strictly prohibited.

LEAVE POLICY
1. Annual Leave
Employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per year. Unused leave can be carried over up to 10 days to the next calendar year.

2. Sick Leave
10 days of paid sick leave are provided. A medical certificate is required for absences longer than 3 consecutive days.

3. Parental Leave
Primary caregivers are eligible for 16 weeks of fully paid leave. Secondary caregivers are eligible for 6 weeks of fully paid leave.

4. Bereavement Leave
Employees are granted up to 5 days of paid leave in the event of the death of an immediate family member.

5. Sabbatical
After 5 years of continuous service, employees are eligible for a 4-week paid sabbatical.

HEALTH BENEFITS
The company provides comprehensive health insurance through HealthPlus 2026 plan.
- Dental and Vision are included.
- Mental health support includes 10 free therapy sessions per year.
- Gym reimbursement up to $50/month.

IT TROUBLESHOOTING
1. VPN Connection Issues
If you cannot connect to the VPN, first restart your Cisco AnyConnect client. If the issue persists, verify your 2FA token. Contact IT Support at extension 5555 if the problem continues.

2. Password Reset
Passwords can be reset via the self-service portal at internal.portal.com/reset. New passwords must be at least 12 characters long and include special characters.

3. Software Installation
Employees do not have admin rights. To install new software, submit a ticket via the IT Service Desk portal. Approval typically takes 24 hours.

PERFORMANCE REVIEWS
1. Cycle
Performance reviews are conducted bi-annually in June and December.

2. Ratings
Ratings are on a 5-point scale: 1 (Needs Improvement) to 5 (Exceeds Expectations).

3. Promotions
Promotion cycles occur in January. Eligibility requires a minimum rating of 4 in the last review cycle.

TRAVEL POLICY
1. Booking
All business travel must be booked through the corporate travel portal (Concur).

2. Per Diem
Daily meal allowance is $75 for domestic travel and $100 for international travel.

3. Flights
Economy class is standard for flights under 6 hours. Business class is permitted for flights over 6 hours.

CODE OF CONDUCT
1. Workplace Harassment
The company has a zero-tolerance policy for harassment of any kind. Reports can be made anonymously via the Ethics Hotline.

2. Social Media
Employees should avoid posting sensitive company information on social media. When posting personal opinions, state that they do not represent the company.
